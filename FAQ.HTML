Common Questions

What types of perfumes do you offer? +

We deal in men's perfumes, women's perfumes, unisex perfumes, and also house perfumes because we care more about your scent. Our collection includes a wide range of fragrances from fresh and floral to woody and oriental notes.

Are your perfumes long-lasting? +

Yes, our perfumes are crafted with high-quality ingredients to ensure long-lasting fragrance. The longevity varies by scent type, with most lasting 6-8 hours on the skin. We recommend applying to pulse points for maximum effect.

What are your shipping options and delivery times? +

We offer standard shipping (3-5 business days) and express shipping (1-2 business days). Orders placed before 2 PM are processed the same day. Delivery times may vary based on your location and current order volume.

Do you offer international shipping? +

Yes, we ship to over 50 countries worldwide. International shipping times vary from 7-14 business days depending on the destination. Additional customs fees may apply for international orders.

What is your return policy? +

We offer a 30-day return policy for unopened and unused products. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, you can return it within 30 days for a full refund or exchange. Perfumes that have been opened can only be returned if defective.

How should I store my perfume? +

To preserve your perfume's quality, store it in a cool, dark place away from direct sunlight and extreme temperatures. Avoid storing in bathrooms where humidity can affect the fragrance. Keep the bottle tightly closed when not in use.

Do you offer sample sizes? +

Yes, we offer sample sizes of most of our fragrances. These are perfect for trying before committing to a full bottle. Sample sets are also available, allowing you to experience multiple scents at a discounted price.
